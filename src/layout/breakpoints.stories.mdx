# Breakpoints

Breakpoints are customizable widths that determine how your responsive layout behaves across device or viewport sizes

## Core concepts

<ul>
  <li>
    <b>Breakpoints are the building blocks of responsive design</b> Use them to
    control when your layout can be adapted at a particular viewport or device
    size.
  </li>
  <li>
    <b>Mobile first, responsive design is the goal</b>. Aims to apply the bare
    minimum of styles to make a layout work at the smallest breakpoint, and then
    layers on styles to adjust that design for larger devices. This optimizes
    your CSS, improves rendering time, and provides a great experience for your
    visitors.
  </li>
</ul>
